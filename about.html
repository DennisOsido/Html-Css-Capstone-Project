Remote Devcon 2020

ANNUAL REMOTE DEVELOPER CONVENTION 2020

Remote work (full-time work-from-home) is supposed to be the one real benefit of the internet. Imagine if all of us could work from anywhere, anytime, however, we want to, without any barriers.

We are advocates of flexible work, work from anywhere policies, sharing economy, diversity & gender balance at work. To make sure there is real value in this conference, we got together a phenomenal panel of speakers. See and judge for yourself.
